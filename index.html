Profile

Clinical precision with an academic and digital horizon.

Dr. Hamdan is an Oberarzt for spine surgery and neurosurgery at Fachklinik 360° in Ratingen. His clinical work brings together structured neurosurgical training in Germany, specialisation in spine procedures, and a patient-centered approach to pain therapy.

His humanitarian work with Médecins Sans Frontières in Amman — treating war-wounded patients from Syria, Yemen, and Iraq — remains a defining influence: technical discipline matters most when paired with resilience, clarity, and compassion.
